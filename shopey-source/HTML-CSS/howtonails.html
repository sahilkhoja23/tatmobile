What is your nails goal?

Soften Cuticles and Strenghten Nails

Cracked cuticles can be both unsightly and painful. Brittle nails that break frequently make the possibility of beautiful hands and feet just a dream. Argan oil is the ultimate nail remedy as it simultaneously works to soften cuticles and fortify nails upon application.

Argan Oil is rich with Vitamin E that supplies nails with the ample moisture they often need. Lack of moisture in hands and nails results in rough cuticles that experience pain when being pushed back during manicures. Dehydrated nails also break easily which creates difficulty when attempting to grow them out. To give nails a radiant look (and great scent), follow our Argan Oil Nail and Cuticle Treatment:

  1. Combine equal amounts of The Argan Tree Pure Argan Oil Serum and freshly squeezed lemon juice in a ceramic bowl.
  2. Soak your fingertips and toes in the solution for 10-15 minutes. While the citric acid is opening the pores of your nails and cuticles, the argan oil is immediately being absorbed.
  3. Rinse your hands and feet and cover with a hot, moist towel until towel cools; pat dry.
  4. Apply 1 pump of The Argan Tree Argan Oil Serum onto each nail and massage deeply into nail and cuticle.
  5. Repeat once a week.

Long-Lasting Manicure and Pedicure

Nails that constantly chip are not only frustrating but also lead to an expensive trip to the salon! If your nails fray when hitting the emery board, it is likely that they are too porous and therefore over-absorb moisture, making them weak. The application of argan oil to nails will restore moisture balance and strengthen nails, providing for a smooth and healthy base. Polished nails frequently experience the opposite and begin to chip from lack of moisture, which can also be prevented with argan oil. For a longer lasting manicure and/or pedicure, try our Argan Oil Mani/Pedi Treatment:

  1. Massage 1 small pump of The Argan Tree Pure Argan Oil Serum into each nail daily.
  2. File nails and apply polish. Let dry.
  3. Apply a bit more Pure Argan Oil Serum as a topcoat to each polished nail. The moisture from the oil will absorb into your painted nails and provide a perfect polish for days to come.
