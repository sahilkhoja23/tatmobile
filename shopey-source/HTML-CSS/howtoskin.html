Oil Control

As someone with oily skin, the last thing you probably want to hear is a suggestion to apply even more oil to it. However, argan oil is known for balancing the production of sebum naturally released by the dermis. Sebum is produced to lubricate and protect our skin from the environment; sebum also tends to be overproduced when skin is lacking in moisture. Therefore, the sufficient application of a natural moisturizer is essential to decrease an oily appearance over time.

To use Argan Oil to Reverse Oily Skin:

  1. Use a gentle exfoliator to cleanse skin of dead cells, dirt, and sebum 3 times per week.
  2. During the day, apply only 1 pump of The Argan Tree Pure Argan Oil Serum. You may also chose to skip this step to avoid unwanted shine.
  3. At night, apply 2-3 pumps of Argan oil on oily skin. Rub in gentle, circular motions and leave overnight.
  4. There is no need for extra cleansing of skin to remove oil in the morning other than your usual shower routine.
  5. Use this remedy 2 times per week and increase gradually over time. Also be careful about your diet and choice of makeup, as these can cause unwanted slickness and shine.
